All of the food preparation is done in your very own kitchen, so you know exactly where your food is coming from. I take care of all of the cleaning, so you can spend more time enjoying dinner.

Meal Prep

After your free consultation, I will customize your weekly/bi weekly meal plan according to your taste and dietary preferences. You will receive a proposed menu for your review. Once the menu is agreed upon, I will prepare all meals in your kitchen to ensure optimal freshness. The meals are packaged in microwave safe containers, and labeled with instructions for reheating. All you need to do is heat the meal in the microwave or oven, and enjoy!
